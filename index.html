Who is Togenashi Togeari

Togenashi Togeari (トゲナシトゲアリ) is a fictional band from the multimedia project Girls Band Cry. Their name carries a contradictory meaning "Without Thorns, With Thorns" or "Thorns Without Thorns" a reflection of pain, emotional scars and the strength of the members who continue to endure despite being hurt.
The band consists of five girls from very different and troubled backgrounds, brought together through music.

Nina, the vocalist, often comes across as abrasive — not because she wants to, but because she's tired of never being trusted. Bullied for standing up for what’s right, her voice carries both pain and defiance.

Momoka, the guitarist, dreams of making music on her own terms, away from expectations and rules. She believes in freedom of expression and fights to protect it.

Subaru, the drummer, wants to escape the weight of her grandmother’s expectations — to be a perfect actress — and just be herself, loud and wild behind the drums.

Rupa, the bassist, lives with the regret of not calling her parents when she still had the chance. Her quiet strength hides a deep sense of loss.

Tomo, the pianist, is cold and perfectionist — not by nature, but shaped by betrayal. Her mother’s infidelity taught her to shield herself with control and logic.

Together, they are more than their wounds. They are Togenashi Togeari raw, real, and unafraid to scream what others are too scared to say.

My Thoughts

What's my thoughts about this band?

Togenashi Togeari is the reason I can get through my problems. Their songs aren't just lyrics they are pure expressions from the heart, longing for someone to talk to but never finding the right person. In the end, I found comfort in their music, which became the best communicator for what I couldn't express.


I always listen to this song whenever I'm sad, happy, or even angry. The lyrics never fail to send shivers down my spine. With deep meaning and a message that reflects my life tired of being seen as the problem, not wanting to disappear in a harsh reality that keeps saying, "You won't make it" "Just surrender" or "It's impossible for you". I try to avoid everything that denies my goals, and this song pours everything into my heart to remind me that I'm not wrong.


I just want to say thanks to them for making good songs that match my vibes plus my inner heart, i hope they have the second season though, please toei
